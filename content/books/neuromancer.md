# Neuromancer

The book that defined cyberpunk.

## Key Themes
- Technology and consciousness
- Virtual reality (before it existed)
- Corporate dystopia
- Artificial intelligence

## Impact
Gibson basically predicted the internet, cyberspace, and hacking culture. Reading this in 2026 is wild because so much of it came true.

The prose is dense but atmospheric. The world-building is incredible even if the plot can be hard to follow at times.

## Characters
- **Case**: Burnt-out hacker protagonist
- **Molly**: Razor-fingered street samurai
- **Armitage**: Mysterious employer
- **Wintermute**: The AI pulling strings

## Rating: 4/5
Essential cyberpunk reading, though the style isn't for everyone.
